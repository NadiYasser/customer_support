# Return & Refund Policy

## Standard returns
You may return most items within **30 days of delivery** for a full refund, provided they
are unused and in their original packaging.

## Sale items
Items bought on sale or with a discount code are returnable within **14 days** and are
eligible for **store credit only**, not a cash refund.

## How refunds are issued
Approved refunds are returned to the original payment method within **5–7 business days**
after we receive the returned item.

## Damaged or defective items
If an item arrives damaged or defective, contact support within **48 hours** of delivery for
a full refund or replacement at no cost — return shipping is on us.
