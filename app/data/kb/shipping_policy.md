# Shipping Policy

## Processing time
Orders are processed within **1–2 business days**. Orders placed on weekends or holidays are
processed the next business day.

## Delivery estimates
- **Standard shipping:** 3–5 business days
- **Express shipping:** 1–2 business days
- Estimated delivery dates shown at checkout are estimates, not guarantees.

## Shipping costs
Standard shipping is **free on orders over $50**. Below $50, a flat **$4.99** fee applies.
Express shipping is **$12.99**.

## Tracking
A tracking number is emailed once your order ships. You can also ask support for the latest
status using your order number.

## International
We currently ship within the country only. International shipping is not yet available.
