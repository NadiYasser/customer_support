# Product, Warranty & Account Policy

## Warranty registration
All products carry a standard warranty. To activate extended coverage you must register your
product within 30 days using the warranty code **WRTY-2YR** printed on the box. Registration is
done from your account dashboard under "My Warranties".

## RMA process
Before shipping any return, request a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number from support.
We cannot accept a return without an RMA number — packages without one are refused at the
warehouse and sent back at your expense.

## AeroDesk Pro 5000 desk
The AeroDesk Pro 5000 (SKU **ADP-5000**) is our flagship standing desk. It supports a maximum load
of 120 kg and ships in two boxes. Replacement parts are ordered by the SKU suffix, e.g. **ADP-5000-LEG**
for a leg assembly or **ADP-5000-CTRL** for the height controller.

## NovaChair ergonomic chair
The NovaChair (SKU **NVC-220**) ergonomic office chair includes a 10-year frame warranty. Casters
and armrests are sold separately as **NVC-220-CAST** and **NVC-220-ARM**.

## COD and payment methods
We accept cash on delivery (COD) for orders under $200 within the metro area only. COD orders
cannot use a discount code and are not eligible for express shipping.

## GDPR data requests
To exercise your GDPR rights (data export or deletion), submit a request from your account under
"Privacy". We respond to GDPR requests within 30 days as required by law.
